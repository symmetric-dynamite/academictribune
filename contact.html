Guidelines

We do not edit your letters, nor do we omit letters. Your voice will be heard. However, we do have a few rules to ensure quality.

  • We must be able to identify your credentials.
  • You must have a Ph.D from an accredited University.
  • The letter should make a contribution to the public debate.
  • We will not impose word restrictions, but please try to keep your letters short.
  • We can post videos in lieu of a letter.
